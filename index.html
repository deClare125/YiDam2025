How We Met

Yi Fang and Adam met on a summer job in May 2018, where, in the wee hours of the night, they caught Cope's Gray Treefrogs in ponds across the Twin Cities for behavioral experiments. It was not in this highly romatic setting, however, that they fell in love. Three months after they met, they went on a 3-week long field class as a part of their degree program, and hit it off from there. Today, Adam and Yi Fang share a cosy apartment together, complete with a Crested Gecko, a Cuban Treefrog, two Mourning Geckos, a fishtank filled with approximately 10,000 scuds, and two cats - Mipha and Impa. They love traveling, hiking, crafting, cafe-crawling and playing Switch games, and look forward to spending the rest of their lives together.
